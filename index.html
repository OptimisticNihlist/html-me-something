Strife, Theocracy, and War

An examination of the fall of the Galactic Republic

The Galactic Republic spanned thousands of worlds for a time period greater than any civilization on Earth. Why would such a grand and expansive civilization fall, you may ask? On this page I will explore the many competing factions, areas of lax governmental control, and failures of this galaxy-spanning republic.
